Trust Agreements

Trust Agreements are simple, shared understandings for confidentiality and responsible access online.

🛡️ Basic NDA v1

This is a universal, lightweight Non-Disclosure Agreement for early access, testing, and respectful collaboration.

  • ✅ Keep private things private.
  • ✅ Don’t share, copy, or reverse-engineer without permission.
  • ✅ Ask first if unsure.

To use it, simply reference:

https://trustagreements.org/basic-v1

or embed in metadata:

<meta name="trust-agreement" content="basic-v1">

nda:basic-v1
